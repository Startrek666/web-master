AI and Security Online Training Course

Online Course is 30 hours training course, which will admit 40 excellent trainees including young researchers, Ph.D., and master students.

Course Overview

Duration

30 hours

Trainees

40 trainees max

Syllabus

  • Basic Mathematic Knowledge

    Learn the foundational mathematics required for AI and security.

  • AI Based on Encrypted Data

    Explore AI models that operate on encrypted data.

  • GAN Models

    Understand Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and their applications.

  • Related Models Analysis

    Analyze models related to AI and security.

  • AI and Security Implementation

    Discover practical implementations of AI in security.

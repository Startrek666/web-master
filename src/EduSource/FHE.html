FHE Online Training Course

Online Course of Fully Homomorphic Encryption would like to introduce the foundation, research trends, and applications including number theory, basic lattice theory, classic schemes analysis of Fully Homomorphic Encryption, and implementation. This is the first systematic course for FHE. This 30 hours training course will admit 40 excellent trainees including young researchers, master, and Ph.D. Every registered trainee will get the whole Fully Homomorphic Encryption theory.

Course Overview

Duration

30 hours

Trainees

40 trainees max

Syllabus

  • Fully Homomorphic Encryption Foundation

    Learn the foundational concepts of Fully Homomorphic Encryption.

  • Classic Schemes Analysis of Fully Homomorphic Encryption

    Analyze classic schemes of Fully Homomorphic Encryption.

  • Homomorphic Encryption Scheme Implementation

    Explore practical implementations of homomorphic encryption schemes.

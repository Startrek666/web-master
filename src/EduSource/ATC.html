ATC Online Training Course

Advanced Topics in Cryptography (ATC) is one of the effective means to understand advanced cryptography and advanced research. It is suitable for senior researchers, Ph.D., and Master students. Online Course is 30 hours training course, which will admit 40 excellent trainees including young researchers, Ph.D., and master students.

Course Overview

  • Duration

    30 hours

  • Trainees

    40 trainees max

Syllabus

  • Fundamentals of Cryptography

    Learn the foundational concepts of cryptography.

  • Secure Multi-party Computation

    Explore techniques for secure computation among multiple parties.

  • Fully Homomorphic Encryption

    Understand fully homomorphic encryption and its applications.

  • Functional Encryption

    Study functional encryption and its use cases.

  • Obfuscation

    Learn about obfuscation techniques in cryptography.

Secure Computation Online Training Course

Secure Computation (SC) is one of the effective means to complete data confidentiality and Users' privacy. SC is widely used in cloud computing, big data, IoT network, etc. The course is suitable for senior researchers, Ph.D., and Master students. Online Course is 30 hours training course, which will admit 40 excellent trainees including young researchers, Ph.D., and master students.

Course Overview

Duration

30 hours

Trainees

40 trainees max

Syllabus

  • Fundamentals of Cryptography

    Learn the cryptographic foundations of secure computation.

  • Private Databases

    Explore techniques for securing private databases.

  • Computing Over Encrypted Data

    Understand methods for performing computations on encrypted data.

  • Anonymity

    Study anonymity techniques in secure computation.

  • Verifiable Outsourcing of Computation

    Learn how to verify outsourced computations securely.

  • Trainee Director

    Guidance and mentorship for trainees.

BL Online Training Course

Blockchains is a very important tool for trusted computation and is the foundation of Cryptocurrencies. This course is suitable for senior researchers, Ph.D., and Master students. Online Course is 30 hours training course, which will admit 40 excellent trainees including young researchers, Ph.D., and master students.

Course Overview

Duration

30 hours

Trainees

40 trainees max

Syllabus

  • Crypto Background

    Learn the cryptographic foundations of blockchain technology.

  • Bitcoin Foundation

    Understand the fundamentals of Bitcoin and its underlying technology.

  • Bitcoin Mining

    Explore the process of Bitcoin mining and its significance.

  • Anonymity

    Study the anonymity features of blockchain systems.

  • Applications of Blockchains

    Discover real-world applications of blockchain technology.

  • Ethereum

    Learn about Ethereum and its role in decentralized applications.
